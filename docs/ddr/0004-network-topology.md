# Network topology: mesh with a heart

Humans find one Gate that leads to a central **nexus** — a Builder megastructure of
Gates that is the safe core, the Central Authority's seat, and the spawn point. But
Gates still dial **point-to-point** out in the network (a mesh), and lawless hubs
sit out in the frontier. Chosen over pure hub-and-spoke, which would break bilateral
raid tunnels and the direct-dial-vs-hub economy. *Proposed; awaiting confirmation.*
