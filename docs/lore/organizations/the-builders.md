# The Builders

The precursor civilization that made the Gates — **unknown by design** (src:
raw/design-overview.md). [decided]

## What is known
- They built the Gate network and then vanished. **Nothing else is established.**
- Who they were and why they built it is hidden from everyone, including the
  [[Central Authority]].

## Why they matter
Their ignorance is deliberate — the long-game **mystery engine**. The [[The Wake]]
(the network switching on) and the rising instability point back to them: the
hidden truth of *what* woke the Gates is the central mystery. Candidate truths are
tracked in open-questions.md. [tentative]

## Open questions
Everything, by design. **Do not invent** their history, names, appearance, or
motives. Resolve only when the mystery payoff requires it.
