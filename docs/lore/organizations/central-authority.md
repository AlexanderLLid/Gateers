# Central Authority

The power that runs the Gates and charters the players. *Placeholder name* —
Directorate / Survey / Commission / Charter Office (open) (src:
raw/design-overview.md). [tentative]

## What it is
It detected [[The Wake]], found the discovery too vast to hold alone, and issues
**charters** to independent operators (the players) to claim and hold Gate-worlds
for a **tithe**. It holds the root of the network, so it can dispense access; it
guarantees claims and polices a safe core. Its writ **stops at the frontier** —
past there, "reach equals exposure" is the only law.

## Why it matters
The in-world authority over Gates and the face of the setting's order. It does
**not** know what truly woke the network (see [[The Builders]], [[The Wake]]).
