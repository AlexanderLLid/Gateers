# Systems Overview

Map of Gaters mechanics. This phase keeps systems **high-level except gate
physics**; the rest stays in raw/ until the phase calls for it (src:
raw/design-overview.md).

## Core idea
**Reach equals exposure** [decided] — you act on the world only as far as you open
to it, you choose when, and you cannot be touched while absent. The aggressor
fantasy (raiding, looting) without the defender tax (babysitting, decay, offline
loss).
