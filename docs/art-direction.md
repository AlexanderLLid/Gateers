# Art Direction (deferred)

Art is **deferred until after greybox validation** [decided]. Captured so it isn't re-litigated; the final identity is open (open-questions #6).

- **Touchstones:** Minecraft, Valheim, No Man's Sky. **NMS is the _aspirational_ visual target**; the Gate structure deliberately sidesteps NMS's hardest engineering problem (seamless space-to-surface flight → replaced by **Gate loads**). **Valheim** is the _achievable_ Unity aesthetic.
- **Principle [decided]:** separate **visual style** (cheap, achievable solo) from the **engine tech** of reference games (NMS's custom engine + seamless flight — not achievable solo).
- **Leaning path to the NMS look solo:** low-poly, flat-shaded 3D with strong post-processing.
- **Open:** final identity (NMS-lush vs. low-poly-stylized), palette, UI style.
