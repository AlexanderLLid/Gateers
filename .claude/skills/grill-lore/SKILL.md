# grill-lore

Pick the target page (or the open questions across recent pages). Interview the
human one decision at a time until the page is consistent and complete:
- Ask about the unresolved fields and open questions, one branch at a time.
- Stress-test answers against existing canon and the timeline; surface
  contradictions as you go (classify per the contradiction-check discipline).
- Write answers back into the page as they are confirmed, with provenance.

Do not invent canon to fill silence; ask. Stop when the open questions are
resolved.
